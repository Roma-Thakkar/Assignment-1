The Three Types of Crcket are Test Cricket, The one day cricket and the twenty twenty.

Test Cricket

Test cricket is the longest form of cricket. Test matched are played for five days. It's a four innings match. So each team plays batting and bowlling twice when necessary. Sometimes the second batting team wouldn't have to bat because the fist team batting couldn't reach the total that the second team lead. The first test was played in the MCG.

One days

One day cricket is a form of cricket with limited overs. There are 50 overs in each innings. The first match of one-day seris was also in the MCG(Melbourne Cricket ground). One day cricket is played in just one day for several hours.

Twenty Twenty

Twenty Twenty is also named as T20. As the ODI(One Day cricket) the T20 has limited over of 20 each innings. T20 goes around for 3 hours. First match was in Eden park in New Zealand.

Competitions around the world are the BBL, IPL, county cricket and more.

BBL

BBL stands for Big Bash League. Big Bash League is series of matches played between different teams of Australia. Some people from oversea comes to join any team. The teams for BBL are Brisbane Heat, Melbourne Star, Melbourne Renegades, Perth scorchers, Adelaide Strikets, Horbat Hurracanes, Sydney Thunder and Sydney Sixers. 2017-18 winners were the Adelaide Strikers. The Big Bash League is played in December and January. There is also womens BBl which is the WBBL. The Wommens Big Bash League. BBL has 20 overs each innings.

IPL

IPL is another version of saying Indian Premier League. The francisers give so many people great amount of money that other cricketers come from everywhere to play the IPL. 2018 IPL final was won by Chennai Superkings won against Hyderbard Sunrisers. The IPL is played in April and May. This is very similar to the Big Bash League. Each match is played for 2 innings with 20 overs. There are a lot of teams in the IPL.

County Cricket

County Cricket is played for 50 overs as one day. It has been played since the 18th century. There are two types of county cricket- County Championship and Minor Counties Championship. In the county campionship it is a first class competition which have eighteen teams, seventeen that are english and one is Wales.There are nineteen english county clubs and one club from the Welsh in the minor counties championship.
